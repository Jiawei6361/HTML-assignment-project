Maldives Tour

21 Jun 2025      7 Days Trip      Maldives



Overview

The Maldives is a tropical paradise, known for its crystal-clear waters, vibrant marine life, and luxurious overwater villas. This 7-day tour offers a perfect blend of relaxation, adventure, and cultural exploration. Whether you're snorkeling with manta rays, indulging in Maldivian cuisine, or enjoying a sunset cruise, every moment will be unforgettable.

Escape to the breathtaking islands and experience the serenity of pristine beaches, turquoise lagoons, and swaying palm tress. This tour is designed for travelers seeking a balance between adventure and tranquility, allowing you to explore hidden gems while enjoying world-class hospitality.


Programme at a Glance

     Duration: 7 Days, 6 Nights
    Departure: 21 June 2025, Kuala Lumpur International Airport (KLIA)
     Arrival: Velana International Airport, Maldives


Tour Highlights

  • Luxury beach front & overwater villa stays
  • Snorkeling & diving with exotic marine life
  • Sunset dolphin-watching cruise
  • Sandbank picnic & beach BBQ
  • Local island cultural tour
  • Water sports: kayaking, paddleboarding, jet skiing
  • Maldivian cooking & traditional dance experience

7-Day Tour Itinerary

Day Activity Overview
1 Arrival in Maldives, welcome dinner by the beach

4 Sandbank picnic & sunset BBQ
