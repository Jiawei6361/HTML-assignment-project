Save Our Environment

Tree planting plays a crucial role in saving our environment by combating climate change and promoting biodiversity. Trees absorb carbon dioxide from the atmosphere, helping to reduce the greenhouse effect and slow global warming. In addition, they provide shelter and food for countless species, improve air quality, and prevent soil erosion. By planting more trees, we contribute to a healthier planet and a more sustainable future for generations to come.

Protecting Our Planet with Trees

Why Planting Trees Matters

Tree planting is vital for a healthy environment. Trees absorb carbon dioxide, improve air quality, and help prevent soil erosion. They also support wildlife and contribute to biodiversity. By planting trees, we protect the planet and create a sustainable future.

Our Mission

Our mission is to restore and protect natural forest landscapes in Malaysia through tree planting. By doing so, we aim to enhance biodiversity, restore ecological functions, and improve the long-term livelihoods of local communities. To achieve this, we focus on reforestation efforts across active plantations in nine provinces throughout Malaysia.

We operate a tree nursery located at our project site in Kuala Lumpur, home to over 50 tree species. Each year, we produce an average of 500 seedlings. Since beginning our tree planting efforts in 2022, we’ve expanded our projects to include initiatives like mangrove restoration, launched in 2024.

Our mission is to planting 10,000 plants within 10 years！！

Tree Planting Day – Success!

On January 1th, 2025, our club hosted another successful tree planting event at our state, as part of our ongoing reforestation efforts in Malaysia. This event saw 90 passionate participants – including 25 local students, 30 volunteers, and 35 foreign students – come together to make a lasting impact on our environment.

In just a few hours, we planted 800 trees, helping to restore vital ecosystems and contributing to the greening of our state. Our efforts are part of a broader mission to promote biodiversity, combat climate change, and improve air quality. This marks another milestone in our goal to plant 10,000 trees, and we're proud to see our community's commitment to environmental sustainability grow stronger with each event.

We would like to thank everyone who participated, and we look forward to continuing our tree planting initiatives, including mangrove restoration projects, to protect and restore Malaysia's rich natural landscapes.

Together, we’re creating a greener, healthier planet!

We look forward to your participation!
