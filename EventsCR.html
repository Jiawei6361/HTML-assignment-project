Colour Run

This event has passed.

21 Nov 2024      12:30 PM - 7:00 PM      Asia Pacific University


A Vibrant Celebration of
Unity and Fitness!

The Colour Run 2024 at Asia Pacific University (APU) was an unforgettable experience filled with laughter, energy, and vibrant colours. Participants embarked on a 3-kilometre route around the campus, passing through multiple colour stations where they were showered with bright, eco-friendly colour powders. This event was not just about running, it was about embracing diversity, building friendships, and celebrating the joy of movement.


Event Details

   Date: 23 November 2024 (Saturday)
   Time: 12:30 PM - 7:00 PM
    Venue: Asia Pacific University

Highlights:

  • Colour splash stations along the 3KM route.
  • Exclusive Colour Run bag (includes two random colour powders, a sports drink, and more surprises!)
  • Delicious food stalls available throughout the event.
  • Option to purchase an official event T-shirt (RM25).
  • Affordable ticket price: RM15 per person.

Rules & Guidelines

Route: A 3-kilometre track around the APU campus
Dress Code: Wear old clothes and comfortable sports shoes (colours will get messy!)

What to Bring:

  • Extra clothing for a quick change after the event.
  • Water bottle to stay hydrated
  • Essential items such as mobile phone and wallet (food stalls will be available!).


Event Kit: Each participant received a Colour Run bag containing:

  • 2 random colour powder packets
  • A refreshing sports drink
  • More surprises! (Stay tuned!)


Ticket Price: RM15 per person
Event T-shirt: Optional purchase at RM25
