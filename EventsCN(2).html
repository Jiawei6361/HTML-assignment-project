14 Dec 2024      3:00 PM - 10:00 PM      Asia Pacific University



A Celebration of Diversity!

On December 14, 2024, Asia Pacific University came alive with a vibrant display of cultural heritage during the annual Cultural Night. The event brought together students from diverse backgrounds to celebrate traditions through mesmerizing performances, authentic global cuisine, and interactive cultural showcases. From traditional dances to music that resonated across continents, the night was a true testament to unity in diversity. The atmosphere was electric, filled with excitement and appreciation for the richness of different cultures.


Event Details

   Date: 14 December 2024 (Saturday)
   Time: 3:00 PM - 10:00 PM
    Venue: Asia Pacific University

Highlights:

  • Traditional Performances: Enthralling dances, music, and theatrical acts from various cultures.
  • Global Cuisine: A feast of international flavors, offering authentic dishes from around the world.
  • Cultural Booths: Interactive displays featuring traditional attire, crafts, and activities.
  • Live Instrumental Showcase: A fusion of classical and modern melodies performed live.
