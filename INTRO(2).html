About Us

The International Student Club was founded to help international students adapt to a new academic and social environment. We understand the challenges of studying abroad—homesickness, cultural differences, and academic adjustments. That’s why ISC serves as a bridge, connecting students with the resources, support, and friendships they need to thrive.

Our club promotes cultural exchange, personal growth, and a sense of belonging. Through our various initiatives, we strive to create a space where everyone feels valued and included.

Mission & Goals

Our mission is to empower international students by providing them with a supportive network, cultural learning opportunities, and resources to help them succeed academically and socially. We aim to create a home away from home for all students.

Foster Cultural Exchange

Encourage students to share and celebrate their cultures with others.

Support Student Well-being

Offer guidance and assistance to help students adjust to life in a new country.

Build a Strong Community

Create opportunities for networking and friendship among students from different backgrounds.
