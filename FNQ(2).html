Frequently Asked Question (FNQ)

Find answers to common questions about our club.
If you still can't answer you're looking for, just Contact Us!

Who can join the club? ▼

Any student, whether local or international, is welcome to join our club and participate in events!

How do I become a member? ▼

You can sign up through our online registration form. Click Here

Are there any membership fees? ▼

No, membership is free! However, some special events may require a small participation fee.

Is there a dress code for club events? ▼

Most events are casual, except some specific events neede to be in semi-formal. Specific dress codes are always noted in event descriptions.

What kind of events does the club organize? ▼

We host cultural nights, holiday celebrations, city tours, and professional networking events. Check our Events page for the current schedule.

How can I stay updated about upcoming events? ▼

You can follow our club's social media pages and check your emails for announcements.
